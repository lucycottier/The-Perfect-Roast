Hey! Welcome to our website about Roast Dinners. We are a group of students and young professionals who wanted to share our love of this amazing dish!

Originating in Britain, this dish is traditionally served on a Sunday afternoon hence why it is otherwise known as “Sunday Lunch”. No matter what day you eat you are sure to be left extremely full and extremely satisfised!
Feel free to browse this site where you can find a handy recipe and the best places for a Roast Dinner in Leeds!

Continued..

The components of a Roast Dinner vary a lot between families and also the areas of Britain.

However, usually the centrepiece of the dish is a roasted meat like beef or chicken, roasted vegetables, stuffing, gravy and of course the best part (in our opinion!) Yorkshire puddings!
